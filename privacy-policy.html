Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

FlowPulse is a minimal productivity app developed by RaimonVibe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required for core functionality:

  • Account data (email, if you create an account for cross-device sync)
  • Usage data (Pomodoro session timers, focus statistics, blocked apps/sites preferences, habit tracking entries)
  • Device information (type, OS version for app compatibility)

No location data, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or other sensitive permissions are accessed unless explicitly granted by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve app features (sync across devices, adaptive Pomodoro, focus reports)
  • Analyze anonymized usage to enhance performance and fix issues
  • Communicate updates or support responses (if you contact us)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

Limited sharing occurs only with:

  • Cloud sync provider (for cross-device functionality, encrypted)
  • Analytics tools (anonymized, aggregated only)
  • Legal requirements (if compelled by law)

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely on encrypted servers. Sync uses industry-standard encryption. We implement reasonable measures to protect against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential analytics
  • Delete your account (erases associated data)

Contact: info@raimonvibe.com

6. Children's Privacy

FlowPulse is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with updated date.
